About Me

I am a games programmer with a background in mainly c#. My experience branches out to other languages such as C++, PHP, HTML, Actionscript, Ajax and a little bit of javascript. I also have experience outside of programming which includes game design, 3D modelling, animation and even a little video editing.

I like to play both rugby union and rugby league when outdoors. Whilst indoors I like to spend my free time watching anime/TV shows and playing video games. Outside of those things my favourite way to spend my free time is working on personal game development projects. Specifically programming the games, however, some elements of design and animation often pop up and can be quite enjoyable.

I currently work part time at Smart Doors and Gates and attend SAE Creative Media Institute to further my skills as a games developer.
