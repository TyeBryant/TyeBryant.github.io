Tye K. Bryant

A prototype for a game with the premise of: Zeke has spent most of his life honing his strength and agility while developing techniques that utilise these attributes to the fullest but at 23 years of age Zeke is struggling to justify having lived this way. In an attempt to find some validation, Zeke enters a battle royale with hopes of rediscovering the reason he started on this path.
